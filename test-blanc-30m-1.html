16. Non-functional requirements (NFRs) must be captured in the Definition of Done.
  • A. True
  • B. False
NFRs (security, performance, etc.) can either fall within the product’s scope, or they can express aspects of product quality. If they are a matter of scope then they should be included on the Product Backlog. If they are reflections of product quality then they may be incorporated into the Definition of Done.
